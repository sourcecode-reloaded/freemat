\section{COSD Cosine Degrees Function}

\subsection{Usage}

Computes the cosine of the argument, but takes
the argument in degrees instead of radians (as is the case
for \verb|cos|). The syntax for its use is
\begin{verbatim}
   y = cosd(x)
\end{verbatim}
